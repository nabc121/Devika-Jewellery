How We Work

From Vision to Jewel

01
Consultation

We begin with a personal consultation to understand your vision, occasion, and budget. Every dream starts with a conversation.

02
Design Draft

Our designers sketch 2–3 concepts for you to choose from. We refine until the design captures exactly what you imagined.

03
Crafting

Master artisans bring the design to life — casting, setting stones, and hand-finishing each element with precision care.

04
Delivery

Your piece is hallmarked, quality-checked, and presented in our signature packaging — ready to be treasured forever.
